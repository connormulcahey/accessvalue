Transaction Advisory

The Axcess Group’s transaction advisory services include independent valuation analysis and due diligence support throughout the deal process.

Pre-closing fairness opinions by our independent valuation professionals provide unbiased and impartial third-party confirmation of a target’s enterprise value, guide shareholder and fiduciary decision-making, and help safeguard shareholder value.

Access Value’s valuation team has also led and participated in corporate and private equity roll-ups and divestitures, and provided financial modeling to deal teams to develop purchase prices and consider various deal structures.

Post-closing, our valuation professionals assist acquiring companies and their audit staff in valuing intangible assets, intellectual property and goodwill for financial reporting purposes.

Our transaction advisory team serves corporate boards, ESOP fiduciaries, private equity and hedge fund investors, and supports legal, tax, and audit teams, from regional and international law firms to the Big 4 and national accounting firms.
