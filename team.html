About Us

Access Value specializes in professional, independent business valuation consulting and financial advisory services.

With an average of 20 years of valuation and corporate development experience, Access Value's accredited appraisers provide our business and professional clients with key valuation information and solutions to access the value in business assets and equity.

Our team of credentialed business appraisers have completed peer reviews, experience requirements, and achieved certifications from leading appraisal organizations, including the American Society of Appraisers and the Institute of Business Appraisers.

Our clients include business owners, corporate executives, investors, and their legal, tax, audit, and transaction advisors. Access Value’s valuation engagements serve these clients with their tax and regulatory compliance issues, financial reporting and transaction requirements, dispute resolution, and a variety of ownership transfer activities.

Carolyn Armbrust

Carolyn Armbrust, MBA, ASA, CBA is the principal and founder of the business valuation and financial advisory firm Access Value. She has achieved the Accredited Senior Appraiser designation from the American Society of Appraisers and the Certified Business Appraiser designation from the Institute of Business Appraisers.

In the course of her career, Ms. Armbrust has conducted over 300 professional valuation engagements of U.S. and international companies. Ms. Armbrust’s experience spans both public and private enterprises, valued from under $1 million to over $250 million, and she has served as an independent valuation advisor to numerous private companies in their securing minority and control level investments from private equity firms and multi-billion dollar public companies.

Previous to Access Value, Ms. Armbrust led buy-side and sell-side investment banking engagements for New-York and Connecticut-based firms specializing in the media, information, and exhibition industries. Earlier in her career, Ms. Armbrust led and participated in M&A valuations and transactions for a division of Reed Elsevier, a $9 billion diversified media and information provider.

Ms. Armbrust holds a Master’s of Business Administration in Strategic Planning and Business Policy from the University of New Haven and a Bachelor of Arts in International Business and Foreign Languages from North Central College.

John A. Cento

Mr. Cento, CPA/ABV, ASA, CFF is a specialist providing business valuation services to clients and professional service firms nationwide. He has been associated with Access Value as a Senior Valuation Consultant since early 2017. Mr. Cento is an Accredited Senior Appraiser with the American Society of Appraisers, Accredited in Business Valuation and Certified in Financial Forensics from the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants.

Mr. Cento has over 18 years of valuation, forensic, and accounting experience. He has been engaged to perform valuations for the purposes of divorce, shareholder disputes, buy-sell agreements, estate and gift tax returns, bank collateral, stock options and warrants, stock appreciation rights, and ESOPs. In addition, his experience in the valuation of intangible assets includes consumer brand names, international distribution rights, domestic licensing rights, industrial patents, contingent earnings, and music copyrights.

Mr. Cento has been the lead non-testifying analyst on several forensic accounting engagements including professional athlete compensation analysis, cardiologist compensation, healthcare provider receivables, healthcare provider valuations, business interruption insurance claim preparation, reconstructed personal financial statements, review of opposing valuation opinions, and the defense of a fraudulent financial reporting and disclosure claim.

Mr. Cento holds an MBA in Finance from the Crummer Graduate School of Business at Rollins College, and a Bachelor of Arts degree, with majors in English and French, Summa Cum Laude, from Franklin College of Indiana.
