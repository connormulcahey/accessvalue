About Us

Access Value specializes in professional, independent business appraisal services, valuation consulting, and financial advisory services.

With an average of 25 years of appraisal, valuation, and corporate finance and development experience, Access Value's accredited business appraisers and credentialed financial professionals provide our business and professional clients with key valuation information and solutions to access the value in business assets and equity.

Our team of credentialed business appraisers have completed and passed appraisal report peer reviews, coursework and experience requirements, and achieved certifications from leading appraisal organizations, including the American Society of Appraisers, National Association of Certified Valuation Analysts, CFA Institute, and the Institute of Business Appraisers.

Our clients include business owners, corporate executives, investors, and their legal, tax, audit, and transaction advisors. Access Value’s valuation engagements serve these clients with their tax and regulatory compliance issues, financial reporting and transaction requirements, dispute resolution, and a variety of ownership transfer activities.

Carolyn Armbrust, ASA, CBA

Carolyn Armbrust, MBA, ASA, CBA is the principal and founder of the business appraisal, valuation consulting, and financial advisory firm Access Value. She has achieved the Accredited Senior Appraiser designation from the American Society of Appraisers and the Certified Business Appraiser designation from the Institute of Business Appraisers.

In the course of her career, Ms. Armbrust has conducted over 450 professional business valuation consulting and appraisal services engagements of U.S. and international companies. Ms. Armbrust’s experience spans both public and private enterprises, valued from under $1 million to over $80 billion, and she has served as an independent valuation advisor to numerous private companies in their securing minority and control level investments from private equity firms and multi-billion dollar public companies.

Previous to Access Value, Ms. Armbrust led buy-side and sell-side investment banking engagements for New-York and Connecticut-based firms specializing in the media, information, and exhibition industries. Earlier in her career, Ms. Armbrust led and participated in M&A valuations and transactions for a division of Reed Elsevier, a $60 billion diversified media and information provider.

Ms. Armbrust holds a Master’s of Business Administration in Strategic Planning and Business Policy from the University of New Haven and a Bachelor of Arts in International Business and Foreign Languages from North Central College.

Travis Flenniken, CFA, ASA, CVA

Travis Flenniken is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA Institute), Accredited Senior Appraiser (American Society of Appraisers), and Certified Valuation Analyst (National Association of Certified Valuators and Analysts).

In his role at Access Value, Mr. Flenniken supports business valuation and litigation support engagements. He brings a distinctive combination of credentialed valuation expertise and firsthand experience as a business owner, having acquired, grown, and sold his own company as a bolt-on acquisition to a private equity-backed platform.

Mr. Flenniken has spent nearly two decades advising business owners and their professional advisors on valuation and transaction matters. His experience includes sell-side and buy-side M&A advisory, valuations for marital dissolution and tax compliance, purchase price allocations, and has been qualified as an Expert Witness in numerous court proceedings, arbitration and mediation matters.

Mr. Flenniken holds a Master of Business Administration from the University of Tennessee at Knoxville and a Bachelor of Science in Finance from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.

Laura K. Sandstrom, ASA

Laura K. Sandstrom is an Accredited Senior Appraiser (American Society of Appraisers), and Fractional CFO.
