Business Valuations, M&A Advisory, Support

Since 2001, Access Value has specialized in providing professional business appraisals, valuation consulting, financial and merger & acquisition advisory services to business owners, corporate executives, and investors and their legal, tax, audit, and transaction advisors.

Access Value’s clients are referred to and select our team of trusted professional appraisers for our qualifications, experience, and expertise:

  • Achieved and Maintain Senior Level Appraisal and Finance Credentials – ASA, ABV, CBA, CVA, and CFA
  • Qualified as Expert Witnesses in Litigation Matters and Alternative Dispute Resolution – Probate Court, Surrogate’s Court, Superior Court, Family Court, SEC Investigations, Arbitration & Mediation
  • Qualified Appraiser and Qualified Appraisal Guidelines Under Title 26 CFR of the IRC
  • Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice, Promulgated by the Appraisal Foundation

Our breadth of experience spans over 25 years in providing professional opinions of value, in domestic and international markets, from the U.S. to the Caribbean, and from Europe to Asia.
