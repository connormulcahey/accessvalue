Business Valuation Consulting

Our typical business valuation engagements involve companies that generate annual revenues of under $5 million to over $50 million, and include closely held corporations, limited liability companies, limited partnerships, family limited partnerships, portfolio companies, reporting units, and income-producing real estate.

Our valuation experts are experienced in developing valuation conclusions under the standards of fair market value, fair value and investment value.

We specialize in valuing non-marketable interests, from a fractional or minority interest basis to shared or absolute control, and with thorough consideration of the internal and external factors affecting the subject interest’s lack of control and lack of marketability.

Conflict Resolution & Litigation Support

Hiring a suitable valuation professional is particularly critical when your appraisal needs require complete independence, qualified experience, and compliance with professional appraisal standards.

Access Value’s conflict resolution and litigation support provides legal professionals with access to, and an understanding of, key valuation issues. Our valuation professionals support successful resolutions for mediation, arbitration, and litigation processes.

Access Value’s credentialed valuation professionals comply with the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP), as promulgated by the Appraisal Foundation, and preferred for many tax, eminent domain, and other legal decisions.

Access Value provides legal counsel with identification and evaluation of financial discovery documents, reviewing opposing expert reports, preparing questions for depositions and examination of opposing experts, preparing expert reports, researching alternative valuation theories, and providing expert testimony.
